[ WHAT IS GEN AI ]

What is Generative AI?

Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems trained to produce new content; images, audio, video, text by learning patterns from vast datasets. Unlike traditional AI that classifies or predicts, generative models create.

  • Learns statistical patterns from massive training datasets
  • Generates novel outputs that mimic learned distributions
  • Powered by architectures like GANs, VAEs, and Diffusion models

[ PROS & CONS ]

The Trade-offs

PROS

  • Speed : Generate hours of creative work in seconds, enabling rapid prototyping and ideation
  • Accessibility : Non-artists can produce professional-quality visuals without technical skill
  • Iteration : Rapidly explore dozens of variations of a concept before committing
  • Cost Reduction : Dramatically lowers production cost for prototyping, mockups, and creative exploration

CONS

  • Hallucinations : Models produce plausible but factually incorrect or visually incoherent outputs
  • Copyright : Training data provenance and IP ownership is legally contested and unresolved
  • Bias : Outputs reflect and amplify biases present in training datasets
  • Quality Control : Generated content requires expert review; errors are subtle and hard to detect
